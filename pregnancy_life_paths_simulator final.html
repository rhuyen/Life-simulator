Life Paths: Pregnancy Decision Simulator

A 40-minute decision-making game about school, work, family, support, money, and parenting responsibilities.

Student Setup

This simulator is designed for classroom discussion. It does not tell students what choice to make. It helps students think through real-life consequences, responsibilities, support systems, and long-term planning.

Suggested pace: 5 min intro, 25 min choices, 5 min outcome, 5 min reflection.
